Quality & Safety

Zero Compromise on Quality and Safety

At SAM India, quality and safety are not compliance checkboxes — they are operational values embedded in every process, every inspection, and every site activity.

Quality Systems

Structured Quality Management

Every project operated under a formal Quality Management System — ensuring consistency, auditability, and delivery to specification.

Quality Management System

ISO 9001-aligned QMS covering all project activities — from material approval to final handover. Structured ITPs, method statements, and inspection records.

Inspection & Testing

Comprehensive ITP with hold-point and witness-point management. Third-party inspection coordination. NABL-accredited lab testing for materials.

Document Control

All quality documents — drawings, specifications, inspection records, test certificates — controlled with version management and audit trail.

Non-Conformance Management

Structured NCR lifecycle — identification, investigation, root cause analysis, corrective action, and verification of closure. Preventive action tracking.

Continuous Improvement

Lessons learned program, periodic quality audits, quality performance metrics, and benchmarking against industry standards.

Materials & Procurement Quality

Pre-qualification of vendors and suppliers. Material approval process with test certificates and third-party verification for critical materials.

Inspection Process

Inspection & Test Plan Workflow

Every construction activity follows a defined ITP — from pre-activity checks to in-process inspections and final acceptance.

01

Pre-Activity

Method statement review. Material approvals. Workforce briefing. Equipment readiness.

02

In-Process

Hold and witness point inspections. Third-party testing. Non-conformance identification.

03

Completion

Final inspection. Test results review. Snag resolution. Handover documentation.

04

Documentation

All records uploaded to QMS. Certificate of compliance issued. Audit-ready file closure.

Safety Culture

Safety is Non-Negotiable

Our safety culture starts at leadership and reaches every worker on every site. Safe execution is not a constraint — it is how we work.

Safety Management System

Site-specific Safety Plans, method statement review, and safety risk assessment integrated into every project mobilization.

Safety Culture & Training

Daily toolbox talks, safety induction for all workers, specialized training for high-risk activities (height work, confined space, excavation).

Incident Reporting & Investigation

Zero-tolerance near-miss reporting culture. Structured accident investigation with root cause analysis and corrective action closure.

Permit-to-Work

Formal PTW system for high-risk activities including hot work, excavation, height work, confined space entry, and lifting operations.
