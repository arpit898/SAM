Project Overview

An early and landmark metro project for SAM India Builtwell, demonstrating the company's capability to deliver complex metro depot and station works in Mumbai's challenging urban environment.

Scope of Work

Civil works for the Aarey Metro Depot and adjacent station on Mumbai Metro Line 1. Depot infrastructure, maintenance buildings, stabling lines, and station civil works.

Engineering Highlights

Complex depot layout in constrained urban site
High-quality station civil works to metro standards
Successful delivery within tight Mumbai Metro schedule

Coordination with Mumbai Metro operational timeline
